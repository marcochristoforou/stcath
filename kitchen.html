Focus on the story behind getting the kitchen at St. Catherines, Exeter.

The kitchen at St. Catherines was pretty much non-existent when we moved in. In retrospect we should have focussed on the kitchen right from the start and moved it forward as fast as possible. In the end we didn't have the kitchen done for more than 6 months (at time of writing it's still not complete).

Extractor Ducting

For extractor ducting there were two choices. Ducting to the exterior if that wasn't possible then a re-circulation kit is availble. The position of our extrator offered a major challenge as there were a lot of pipes in the way and we wanted to minimise the visiual impact of any ducting. For a while we thought we were going to have to go with the re-circulation kit; then I had a brainwave.

With a slight deviation to the right...

We could then go straight up through the first floor airing cupboard located in the bathroom...

Then into the roof cavity where we do a right angle turn to head for the front exterior wall...

And finally through the wall...
